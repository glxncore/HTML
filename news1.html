🌍 Scientists Discover New Coral Reef



Scientists used advanced underwater drones and sonar mapping technology to explore the area. The expedition revealed colorful coral formations,
rare fish species, sea turtles, and other marine life that had never been documented before. Researchers believe the reef could play an important role
in understanding marine biodiversity and protecting endangered species. Conservation groups have called for the area to be designated as a protected
marine reserve to prevent damage from fishing and pollution.
