🏗️ City Council Approves New Public Transportation Project

The City Council has approved a new public transportation project to improve travel across the city. The plan includes introducing electric buses, building dedicated bus lanes, and upgrading existing bus stations to provide faster and more reliable transportation for commuters. Construction is expected to begin later this year and will be completed in phases. City officials believe the project will reduce traffic congestion, lower air pollution, and encourage more people to use public transport, creating a cleaner and more sustainable city.
