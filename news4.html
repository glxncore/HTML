🌳 Mayor Announces Plan to Increase Green Spaces in the City

The mayor has announced a new initiative to increase green spaces by developing parks, planting thousands of trees, and creating community gardens across the city. The project aims to improve air quality, provide recreational areas, and promote a healthier environment for residents. The initiative is expected to begin later this year, with several neighborhoods receiving new parks and walking trails. City officials believe the project will enhance the quality of life, encourage outdoor activities, and make the city more environmentally friendly.
